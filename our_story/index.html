Maddie and Scott's Story

Maddie and Scott first met at Carnegie Mellon University in the Fall of 2009. Scott was in his freshman year and lived in Donner on the east side of campus.

Maddie lived in her Donner during her freshman year (2008-2009). In her sophmore year she signed up for Donner Mentors, a program where Donner upperclassmen paired with freshman to mentor them through college. Scott's Housefellow Hellen Wang paired the two of them as mentor and mentee. What Maddie didn't know until later is that Scott specifically requested Maddie as his mentor because he thought she was really cute and funny.

Scott and Maddie (Mott) became friends throughout the 2009-2010 academic year. They both applied to be RAs in Stever house and were accepted. They started dating in the Spring of 2011 on 3/14. To this day pi and pie remain a critical slice of their relationship.

Scott and already been in love with Maddie for at least a year and now it was time for Maddie to fall in love with Scott. It's hard to say what she found more attractive: his hilarious witty banter or his athletic prowess. Whatever it was she fell hard and soon they were a power couple on campus.

Some of their favorite dating spots during college were Phi Bar, (which closed this year triggering a period of mourning on campus), Flagstaff Hill, the Natural Science museum, and of course, Phipps conservatory.

After college Scott successfully convinced Maddie to move to Chicago with him when he got a job offer at eSpark Learning where he currently works as a Data Scientist. Maddie got a job teaching 10th Grade Geometry at DRW Noble Charter School. Maddie started out hating Chicago but now she loves its bitter cold winters and flat, pancake-esque landscape. Who needs mountains?

Then in April of 2014 Scott and Maddie went back to campus for Carnival, CMU's alumni weekend where he proposed at Phipps conservatory.
